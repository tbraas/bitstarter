Do you have a TV? Do you have a Blu-ray/DVD player? Do you have a PlayStation 3? Do you have a Boxee Box? Do you have an unlisted device type that is controlled with an IR remote control? I bet you do! Are you tired of all the remote controls you need to control all your devices? Are you tired of you Logitech Harmony remote control that doesn't work with your non-IR devices? Are you tired of all the broken promises? I bet you are! So am I! So much so I decided to do something about it!

There are two different attachments available for the iPhone. The Beamer is a simple and inexpensive IR based device that plugs into the heaphone outlet on your iPhone or iPod (5G or later). We are currently not planning on a version for Android as the testing effort in order to get adequate test coverage is beyond the scope of this initial project. An Android version will be considered once sufficient numbers of Android users run Android version 4.0 (ICS - Ice Cream Sandwich) or later.
